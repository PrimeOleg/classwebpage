Luca's Notes for Will's lessons

First Lesson (20/09/2023)

First lesson involved creating a list of ammendments when using a computer and evoultion of technology and how it changed during lockdown
The examples of technology which has changed were softwares like: Seneca, Teams, word and in general more people using games to relax.
could be considered as an introductory lesson to emerging issues

Second Lesson (27/09/2023)

Second Lesson involved studying technological changes in the work environment and how the technology changed the way people work
we had to conduct a research on which legislation have been introduced due to technological advancements in a specific sector such as:
Medicine, Delivery, Retail and so on... part of it involved creating a roadmap in canva for a specific company and how it changed according to technology

Third Lesson (Project road signs and Project Party)

The third lesson involved creating and planning a project for changing the 30mph road signs across the whole England with and without the external illumination and how to enforce and communicate the following changes to the people
In addition to that we had to plan a party and decide who to invite, what to order, where to organise it and what to wear.

Fourth Lesson (What makes up a computer)

Fourth Lesson involved discussing things that make up a computer such as CPU's, GPU'S, how to get rid of various computer parts such as printers, laptand in general how long they take to decompose and what impact they have on the environment
we also had to discuss the evoulution of the intel processors and how much they evolved throughout the years and how much powerful they became

Fifth Lesson (Business Policies)

Throughout the fifth lesson we had to research similar business policies to the act called "Investigatory Powers Act 2016"
in addition to that we had to discuss what they do and what are they implemented for in the different businesses such as amazon, meta, google and many more.

Sixth Lesson (Internet of Things)

Throughout the sixth lesson we had to discuss the advantages of the things being connected to the internet and how it can benefit the people
and make their life easier and at the same time how it can make their life harder due to disadvantages
In addition to that we had to create an Infographic for the house of the future where all of the things are connected to the internet
and how they are all connected to the internet in order to make the house function properly.

Seventh Lesson (Future Job)

Throughout the seventh lesson we discussed what jobs will appear in the future and in general how the jobs in the digital sector will change in the upcoming years we also had to do the Employability skills programme by the end of the lesson and create a job specification
which is supposed to be introduced in the future with details of the salary what skills will be needed and for what type of person the job is intended
